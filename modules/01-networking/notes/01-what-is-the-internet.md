# Lesson 01 - What is the Internet?

## Objectives
- Understand what the Internet is.
- Understand why it was created.
- Understand that it is a network of networks.

## Theory
The Internet is a global network of interconnected networks that communicate using standardized protocols such as TCP/IP. It is decentralized and operated by thousands of organizations.

## Key Takeaways
- Internet = Network of Networks
- Uses common protocols
- Foundation of every distributed system

## Reflection
1. Why is the Internet not owned by one company?
2. What problems did networking solve?
3. What components lie between your browser and a server?
